About CanadaConnect

CanadaConnect is a specialized tool designed to help Vancouver Island businesses identify sales opportunities and potential customers across Canada. By analyzing your products and services, we match you with prospective buyers in your target provinces. As interprovincial trade barriers continue to fall and Canadian businesses look to strengthen domestic supply chains, CanadaConnect provides the tools you need to capitalize on these opportunities.
